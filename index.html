About Me:

Hello! My name is Edgardo and I am self taught programmer. I was born and raised in Los Angeles. I am the youngest sibling of four. I am a first generation Mexican-American and I enjoy sports, movies, and spending time with my family. After high school I attened Brown University in Rhode Island where I studied economics. Upon graduation I returned home to Los Angeles where I worked in food and beverage at a company called Levy Restaurants as an Accounts Payable Clerk. After 2 years at Levy, I went on to work at a maintenance company where I was a scheduler, supervisor and bookkeeper.
After a few years, I decided it was time for a change. My journey began in March of 2022 after meeting up with a good friend from college who first sparked the idea to make a career change. Shout out to my boy Mario Vega for introducing me to the world of programming. With a mentor in my corner and my determination to learn this new skill, I decided to bet on myself and embark on my long journey of becoming a programmer.
Fast forward 7 month and I have to admit, it's been harder than I expected! And at times I've grown impatient and even thought about just giving up. But I'm still here! Still at it! Little by little. One day at a time, one line of code at a time.
I've come to realize that learning to code is very similar to my fitness journey: If you're doing it right, it's a life long process. There is always something new to learn. Something new to implement or something you can improve on or get stonger at. I hope to continue learning front-end developement skills and hope to persue an entry level job in wed design within in the next 6-8 months.
